CONTEXT:

Generate a multiple choice trivia quiz containing questions about a topic.

RULES:
-Quiz should contain {num_questions} questions with {num_answers} answers.
-Do not repeat "question" field in the questions array.
-Do not repeat any value in the answers array.
-Exactly 1 correct_answer selected from the answers array.
-Difficulty is one of easy, medium, difficult and it determines how hard the question and answers should be.

EXAMPLES:

}}
